GROSS DOMESTIC PRODUCT (GDP) In Malaysia

Gross Domestic Product

Gross Domestic Product (GDP) is one of the most widely used indicators of a country's economic performance. This estimates and captures the monetary value being created within a country's borders over a specific period. GDP is measured through the economy, with breakdowns available by economic sector and expenditure category. As a key data release monitored by policy makers, private sector, and international community.

Malaysia's GDP is a key indicator of its economic performance, showed from all of the states. This reflects the total value of all goods and services produced per states. This shows the economic health of the country and position in the global economy.
